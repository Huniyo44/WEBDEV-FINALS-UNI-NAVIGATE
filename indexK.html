About Uni-Navigate

Uni-Navigate is a comprehensive wayfinding platform designed specifically for OLFU students. We understand that navigating a large university campus can be challenging, especially when you're trying to find your classrooms, discover the perfect spot to hangout, or locate a quiet area during breaks. Our mission is to make your campus experience smoother by providing clear directions, building layouts, and detailed information about all campus facilities. Whether you're a freshman or a returning student, Uni-Navigate is here to help you explore and master your campus with confidence.

About Us

This website was developed by Labao and Lhance to make the campus easier and to help their fellow OLFU students. Uni-Navigate began as a student project to simplify campus navigation, helping users quickly find classrooms, hangout spots, and essential locations. Our goal is to make every day on campus less stressful and more efficient for students who want to move around with confidence.
